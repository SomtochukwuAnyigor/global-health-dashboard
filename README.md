This interactive dashboard analyzes the global landscape of **Infant and Young Child Feeding (IYCF)** indicators, specifically focusing on the correlation between **Exclusive Breastfeeding (EBF)** rates and **Chronic Malnutrition (Stunting)**. 

### ü©∫ Why This Matters (Clinical Context)
As a medical student, I designed this tool to bridge the gap between population-level data and clinical pathophysiology. 
* **Pathology Focus:** Highlighting the "Immunological Gap" caused by low EBF, which increases vulnerability to enteric infections and subsequent linear growth retardation.
* **Pharmacology Focus:** Addressing how stunting and protein-energy malnutrition affect pediatric pharmacokinetics (drug metabolism and distribution).

---

## üöÄ Key Features
* **Interactive Geospatial Mapping:** Visualization of global EBF rates using Plotly.
* **Clinical Insight Engine:** Automated medical commentary based on country-specific data.
* **Health KPI Metrics:** Real-time tracking of breastfeeding and stunting prevalence.
* **Cross-Country Comparison:** Benchmarking peer nations (e.g., Nigeria vs. Ghana) to identify successful community health models.
